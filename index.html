FAQ

What types of packages can I send?

You can send various packages, including documents, small parcels, boxes, and larger freight shipments, depending on the courier service.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary depending on the service type and destination. Standard shipping may take 3–7 days, while express options typically deliver within 1–3 days. International deliveries might take longer. Check with the courier for exact estimates.

Do you offer business courier services?

Yes, many courier services offer business solutions, including bulk shipping, scheduled pickups, and tailored logistics for companies. These services often include tracking, faster delivery options, and discounted rates for regular use.

What if my package is damaged during delivery?

If your package is damaged during delivery, immediately contact the courier service and report the issue. Most services offer insurance or liability coverage for such cases, allowing you to file a claim for reimbursement or compensation. Keep photos of the damage and relevant documents as evidence.

How can I schedule a pickup?

To schedule a pickup, visit your courier's website or mobile app, log into your account, and select the "Schedule Pickup" option. Provide the pickup address, package details, and preferred date and time. Alternatively, you can call customer service to arrange it.
